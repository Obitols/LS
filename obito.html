OBITO UCHIHA

Obito Uchiha, also known as the Masked Man, is a conflicted but beloved fictional character in the fan-favorite manga and its anime adaptation, the Naruto series that is written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto.Obito was born and grew up in the Hidden Leaf Village and eventually became a member of a genin squad consisting of Kakashi Hatake, Rin Nohara, and himself led by Minato Namikaze. Although his abilities may have faded in comparison to Kakashi in his early years as a shinobi, he surpassed his predecessors on many levels during his prime.Obito was believed to have died in the Third Great Ninja War but he was rescued by Madara Uchiha, also known as the Ghost of the Uchiha. After he witnessed the death of Rin, his love interest, he joined forces with Madara and soon took his identity to realize their dream of a peaceful world by using the Infinite Tsukuyomi.

Obito was born as a member of the legendary Uchiha clan of the Village Hidden in the Leaves. Obito lived with his grandmother in his early years but other than that, information regarding his parents and immediate family are still shrouded in mystery.

At the Five Kage Summit, Obito declared war on the world, the Fourth Great Ninja War, after the Five Kage declined to peacefully hand over Naruto Uzumaki and Killer B, the jinchuriki of the Nine-Tailed and Eight-Tailed Beast respectively. In the war, Obito revived the Ten-Tails and eventually became its Jinchuriki.

Obito's hair turns completely white once he is transformed into the Ten-Tails' jinchuriki and gained an Otsutsuki-like appearance. His skin turns white, ten protrusions—five near his shoulders, five near his hips—appear from his back,and nine smaller ones grouped in rows of three below it—appear on his back in a black array.

At some point, Team Minato participated in the Chunin Exams where Obito was defeated by Might Guy in the third round. Later, Kakashi defeated Might Guy and was promoted to chunin and received Rin’s admiration. To gain Rin’s praise and attention, Obito trained rigorously and eventually got promoted to chunin himself, but his excitement soon ended when Kakashi got promoted to jonin.Obito had now lost faith in the cruel world they lived in, where a pure soul like Rin had to die at the hands of her friend.

Kakashi instructed Obito to leave Rin behind as completing the mission was more important than concerning themselves with Rin’s well-being. Obito was furious at the thought of abandoning Rin and insisted they concentrate on her rescue. Obito walked out by himself after Kakashi refused, stating that a shinobi who abandons his comrades is worse than scum.To his surprise, he was saved by Kakashi who was moved by his words and changed his mind. Unfortunately. Kakashi lost his left eye trying to defend Obito.

Obito had a rough start in his journey to be the strongest shinobi. He managed to graduate from the Hidden Leaf Ninja Academy and became a member of a genin squad. This genin squad consisted of Obito, Kakashi, and Rin, captained by Minato Namikaze. In the anime, the newly formed genin squad had to go through the bell test. Obito was unable to acquire the bell on his own but later succeed in retrieving one by joining forces with Rin and Kakashi and learning the importance of teamwork.
