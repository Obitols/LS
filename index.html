The Uchiha Clan is one of the four noble clans of the Hidden Leaf Village. They possess a powerful Kekkei Genkai called the Sharingan and have incredibly strong prodigies in every generation. The Uchiha Clan are descendants of Indra Otsutsuki. However, after a certain tragedy struck, The Uchiha were blamed for it by the villagers and were distrusted, eventually leading to the downfall of the Uchiha Clan.The Uchiha Clan members are descendants of Indra Otsutsuki, the older son of Hagoromo Otsutsuki. He waged war with his brother, Asura Otsutsuki, for being their father’s successor.
