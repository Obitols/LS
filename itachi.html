ITACHI UCHIHA

Itachi was the first born of Fugaku and Mikoto Uchiha, and he was born during an era of conflict. He was only four years old at the time of the Third Great Ninja War. One day, Fugaku took young Itachi to a battlefield where he witnessed mountains of corpses who died in the war. Fugaku took him to the battlefield to familiarize his son with the cruel life of a shinobi. He there saw a shinobi of the nation asking for some water. When he tried helping the wounded shinobi, he attempted to kill Itachi after he saw his ninja gear.Witnessing such a brutal scene traumatized young Itachi and he became a pacifist. He then decided to become a strong ninja capable of eradicating all conflicts and unnecessary bloodshed from the ninja world and bringing peace and stability. To achieve his goal, he started training himself ruthlessly.

He soon got accepted into the Ninja Academy based on his exceptional skills when he was six years old. He excelled in all fields at the academy and was recognized as his generation's best. After just four months at the academy, his teachers decided to let him take the graduation exam early based on his advanced skills that could match the skill level of a genin. He graduated from the academy at the age of 7.

Itachi was promoted and became a member of Anbu Black Ops. He was the youngest Hidden Leaf shinobi to achieve Anbu status. He was given a position in Team Ro, an elite Anbu force directly under the command of the Hokage of the Hidden Leaf. He served as a member of Team Ro and collaborated with Tenzo (Yamato), and Kakashi Hatake assumed the role of their captain.

Itachi left the village after the Uchiha Clan Massacre and enlisted in the Akatsuki, an organization he saw as a danger to the Hidden Leaf. The criminal group Akatsuki was made up of misfit ninjas with special skills from different hidden villages. The ultimate objective they aimed toward was global dominance.

In his final confrontation with Sasuke, Itachi reveals the secret of the Eternal Mangekyo Sharingan. He then battles and makes Sasuke exhaust all of his chakra. He consented to this task provided his younger brother Sasuke Uchiha would be protected and cared for.
